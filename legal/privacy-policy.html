Privacy Policy for Office Syntax Highlighter

Effective Date: October 27, 2024

1. Introduction

Office Syntax Highlighter is a Microsoft PowerPoint add-in designed to assist users in highlighting and syntax highlighting code within PowerPoint presentations. We prioritize user privacy and take great care to ensure that no personal or presentation data is collected, stored, or transmitted by this add-in.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Office Syntax Highlighter does not collect, process, or store any data. The add-in operates locally within your PowerPoint application and solely provides code formatting functionality. No data, including presentation content, usage patterns, or personal information, is gathered or transmitted by the add-in.

3. Third-Party Services

This add-in does not integrate or share data with any third-party services, APIs, or external systems. As no data is collected, there is no information to share with any third-party entities.
